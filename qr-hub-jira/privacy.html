QR Hub for Jira

Privacy
Policy

Effective date: 1 June 2026 · Last updated: 1 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Agilva Solutions (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles information in connection with the QR Hub for Jira app (“the App”), available on the Atlassian Marketplace.

1. What data does the App access?

QR Hub for Jira is built on Atlassian Forge. All computation and storage occurs within Atlassian’s infrastructure. No data is transmitted to servers operated by Agilva.

To provide its features, the App reads the following data from your Jira Cloud instance — exclusively within Atlassian’s platform:

  • Issue fields: key, summary, type, status, priority, reporter, assignee, description, and dates (for QR panels and print layouts).
  • Board name and ID (for board and backlog QR generation).
  • Project name and key (for project context in QR panels).

None of this data is stored by the App, with the exception described in section 2.

2. What data does the App store?

The App uses Atlassian Forge’s storage:app API to persist one item only: the last URL entered in the Dashboard Gadget. This is stored solely to allow the gadget to restore your QR code across page refreshes.

This stored URL:

  • Remains entirely within Atlassian’s Forge infrastructure.
  • Is scoped to your Atlassian site — it is not accessible to Agilva or any third party.
  • Is a Jira URL only — no personal data is stored.
  • Can be cleared at any time by entering a new URL or removing the gadget from your dashboard.

3. Does any data leave Atlassian?

No. QR Hub for Jira makes no external network calls. There are no external APIs, no analytics services, no crash reporting services, and no external storage. The App is entirely self-contained within Atlassian Forge.

4. Contact form data (agilva.com website)

If you contact Agilva via the contact form on our website, we collect your name, work email, company name, and message. This information is used solely to respond to your enquiry. We use Formspree to deliver submissions to our inbox. We do not use this data for marketing without your explicit consent, and we do not share it with third parties.

5. Cookies

The App itself does not set any cookies. Our website (agilva.com) does not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics.

6. Children’s privacy

QR Hub for Jira is a business productivity tool intended for use by organisations. It is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your rights

Because Agilva does not receive or store any data from the App, there is no personal data held by Agilva to access, correct, or delete. For data held within your Atlassian instance, please refer to Atlassian’s own Privacy Policy and your organisation’s Atlassian administrator.

If you submitted a contact form on our website and wish to have that data removed, email privacy@agilva.com.

8. Third-party services

QR Hub for Jira uses no third-party services of any kind within the App. The App runs exclusively on Atlassian Forge. Atlassian’s own privacy practices govern the Forge infrastructure — see Atlassian’s Privacy Policy.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. For significant changes, we will notify users via the Atlassian Marketplace listing.

10. Contact

For any questions about this policy, contact us via the form or email privacy@agilva.com. We respond within one business day.
