Features

QR codes woven into
every part of Confluence

From the page byline to the editor, QR Hub meets your team wherever they work in Confluence.

Content Action

Every Confluence page and blog post gets a QR Hub option in the ··· actions menu. Opens a modal with the QR code, page metadata, and one-click download in your choice of style, size, and format.

Inline Macro

Type /QR Hub in the Confluence editor to embed a live QR code directly in any page. Configure the target URL, style, and size — then drag the corner handle to resize. On publish, only the QR code is visible.

Byline Badge

A QR Code badge sits in the page byline alongside contributor and date metadata — on every page, with no setup required. Click it to open a compact popup and scan or download instantly.

Global QR Generator

Accessible from Apps → QR Hub in the Confluence nav. Paste any URL — Confluence or otherwise — and generate a QR code instantly. Supports all four styles, four download sizes up to 1024px, and PNG, JPEG, and SVG formats.

Download in Multiple Formats

Export any QR code as PNG, JPEG, or SVG at four sizes: 128px, 256px, 512px, or 1024px. SVG scales infinitely for print — PNG and JPEG are ready for documents, slides, and email.

QR Styles

Choose from Rounded, Classy, or Square dot styles on every generated QR code. Match the aesthetic of your documentation, branded materials, or print layouts.
